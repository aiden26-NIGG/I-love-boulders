How to Play

How It Works

Buy a Wall:

  • Click the "Buy Wall" button (cost: 200 points).
  • A wall will spawn at a random position at the top of the screen.

Walls:

  • Walls block boulders and take damage when hit.
  • Walls are removed when their HP drops to 0.

Boulders:

  • Big boulders deal 2 lives of damage to the player and 20 damage to walls.
  • Small boulders deal 1 life of damage to the player and 10 damage to walls.

Upgrades:

  • Extra Life: Buy an extra life for 100 points.
  • Shield: Activate a shield for 5 seconds for 50 points.
  • Wall: Buy a wall for 200 points.

Press any key to start the game!
